##### The Dot set the foundation of protection, personal & professional support
Like so many, my wife and I have taken the next step, bringing the Ring-Echo systems into our home. Between our Ring front door camera, indoor camera, outdoor camera, and of course the Dot, we feel covered. We took this step after a 3:30 am pounding on our front door. When looking out and not being able to see who it was, only two dark shadows very close to the door and looking back at us, protecting us on another level changed from consideration to we're buying. The Dot was an Amazon good deal giving us the basic options. (Note: the Dot itself does not have a camera, but it works in combination with the Echo Show 8 and Ring cameras we also got, allowing us another room where we can stay on top of things without having to physically be there). Of the option, one of our most often used parts is an alarm that goes off every half hour from 9 am until 7 pm. It keeps us aware of time as we both work at home where it's easy to not pay attention to the time, ending up missing lunch, taking a break, or whatever. We liked the Dot so much we ended up adding an Echo Show 8 for a more expanded range of options (photos, viewing things). With that we moved the Dot to the bedroom, the Echo Show 8 is in the living space, and the Ring cameras are in a few spots. Our home is covered. And while this review is specific to the Dot, it's the fact we have been able to use it on its own, making us happy enough we ended up adding the Echo Show 8, along with the Ring cameras, and increased our safety and comfort. For the most part these are pretty easy to learn how to operate. Between the Dot - App - Show 8 we've gotten a lot of it down, but I admit, we're still figuring things out. We believe the Dot - along with the others - has added protection, the ability to check in whenever we want - even while working at home - can see what's going on outside without having to look out the window or opening the door, and it has perks (reminder to drink water when coughing, time and weather, music, photos, reminders for things, etc). In other words, the Dot has been a great find and the foundation to our sense of safety.
