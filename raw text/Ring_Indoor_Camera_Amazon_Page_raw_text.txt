##### The Perfect Kitty Cam!
This ring indoor cam is a game changer! I caught an Amazon “deal” and bought 2 cams for the price of one. I recently adopted a senior cat and 2 days later, he was hospitalized with a blocked urethra. It is imperative to monitor his litter box habits, because he is at risk for another blockage. I need to know if he is straining or entering the box an unable to produce urine. This guy is often in stealth mode and I don't catch him in the act. I had to update the software before I could set up the cam, but once this was accomplished, the rest was a breeze. I programmed the cam to alert me when the cat uses the litter box. It has been reliable and I have discovered that my cat sometimes simply casually inspects his boxes (he has two) without any intent to use them. Both litter boxes sit side by side in a spare bathroom (in the shower). I used painter's tape to attach the cam to a toilet paper stand. (The cam is super lightweight). I also had to feed an extension cord to the shower entry as the USB cable that comes with the cam was just too short to span across the room. So, I also used painter’s tape to secure the USB cord to the floor. This cam does not have a light, but I leave a night light plugged in. When the ambient light is low, the images sometimes record in black and white, but when the room is brighter, they are in color. The cam has a wide-angle lens (they all do) and this cam is positioned close to the litter boxes, so there is some image distortion (which is to be expected) - I am a photographer, so I notice this more than most. The audio is good enough that I can hear if his stream of urine is weak or strong. I am glad I have the spare cam. I will use this to monitor his food consumption if I am away for a day. It is also nice to know that I have a backup if the first cam malfunctions. The trial subscription was incredibly useful. I wanted to know what I would be paying for. Sometimes when I access my videos from my computer the play back is glitchy. It is NOT glitchy when I access the videos from my phone and I have had no difficult with downloading the videos. I will definitely subscribe for the year-long standard plan when the trial is over. First 2 clips were taken in low light conditions, third clip was taken with ambient daylight. The last photo was taken with my cell phone to show the set up.
