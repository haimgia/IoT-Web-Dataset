Explains what information we collect and why, how we use it, and how to review and update it.
[Read our Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en)

Describes the rules you agree to when using our services.
[Read our Terms of Service](https://policies.google.com/terms?hl=en)
# Google Safety Center
![](https://www.gstatic.com/policies/images/shields2.png)
Making products for everyone means protecting everyone who uses them. Visit safety.google to learn more about our built-in security, privacy controls, and tools to help set digital ground rules for your family online.
[Explore what we do to help keep you safe](https://safety.google/?hl=en)
# Google Account
![](https://www.gstatic.com/policies/images/my_account.png)
Control, protect, and secure your account, all in one place. Your Google Account gives you quick access to settings and tools that let you safeguard your data and protect your privacy.
[Visit your Google Account](https://account.google.com/?hl=en)
# Our Privacy and Security Principles
![](https://www.gstatic.com/policies/images/privacy_security_answers.png)
We build privacy that works for everyone. It’s a responsibility that comes with creating products and services that are accessible for all. We look to these principles to guide our products, our processes, and our people in keeping our users’ data private, safe, and secure.
[Explore our Privacy and Security Principles](https://safety.google/principles?hl=en)
# Google Product Privacy Guide
![](https://www.gstatic.com/policies/images/product_privacy.png)
As you use Gmail, Search, YouTube, and other products from Google, you have the power to control and protect your personal information and usage history. The [Google Product Privacy Guide](https://policies.google.com/technologies/product-privacy?hl=en) can help you find information about how to manage some of the privacy features built into Google's products.
