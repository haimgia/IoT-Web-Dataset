Privacy at Microsoft
##  Your data is private at work, at home, and on the go 
![A collage of graphical user interface elements, including message bubbles, notification panels, and app screens. The elements join together in the center to form the Microsoft logo.](https://cdn-dynmedia-1.microsoft.com/is/image/microsoftcorp/MSPrivacy_HeroBanner_CROP_4000x1250?scl=1)
Our commitment to privacy
We ground our privacy commitments in strong data governance practices, so you can trust that we'll protect the privacy and confidentiality of your data and will only use it in a way that's consistent with the reasons you provided it. 
![A stylized graphic of a user interface with a light blue and green color scheme. It features a document titled ‘Design for storytelling’ with an image of clouds and mountains, and icons for comments, like, and share below.](https://cdn-dynmedia-1.microsoft.com/is/image/microsoftcorp/MSPrivacy_01_CONTROL_NEW_2000x2000?wid=570&hei=570&resSharp=1)
####  You control your information 
We give you the ability to control your data, along with clear and meaningful choices over how your data is used. 
![A group of colorful applications and icons, wrapped in semi-transparent, ribbon-like structures.](https://cdn-dynmedia-1.microsoft.com/is/image/microsoftcorp/MSPrivacy_02_IMAGERY_PROTECTION_NEW_2000x2000?wid=570&hei=570&resSharp=1)
####  Your data is protected 
We rigorously protect your data using encryption and other security best practices. 
![Graphic depicting computer interface elements, including applications and files](https://cdn-dynmedia-1.microsoft.com/is/image/microsoftcorp/MSPrivacy_03_DESIGN_NEW_2000x2000?wid=570&hei=570&resSharp=1)
####  You can expect privacy by design 
We design our products with a core commitment to uphold user privacy. 
![Graphic depicting an organizer that contains documents and other privacy related user information.](https://cdn-dynmedia-1.microsoft.com/is/image/microsoftcorp/MSPrivacy_04_LAW_NEW%202171x2171?wid=570&hei=570&resSharp=1)
####  We stand up for your rights 
We fight for stronger privacy laws and protections, and will protect your rights if a government request is made for data. 
Discover and control your data
Privacy is at the center of how we build the products and services that customers use every day. See privacy resources and controls below where you can manage your data and how it is used. 
![A stylized document or card with a header section, three horizontal lines suggesting text content, and a orange toggle](https://cdn-dynmedia-1.microsoft.com/is/image/microsoftcorp/privacy_dashboard_card_809X461?wid=834&hei=470&fit=crop&resSharp=1)
####  Visit your privacy dashboard 
The privacy dashboard is where you can manage your privacy settings and manage your data for your Microsoft account. 
[ Go to your Privacy Dashboard ](https://account.microsoft.com/privacy)
![A blue shield with a white checkmark in the center](https://cdn-dynmedia-1.microsoft.com/is/image/microsoftcorp/privacy_account_checkup_card_809X461?wid=834&hei=470&fit=crop&resSharp=1)
####  Account check-up 
The account check-up wizard is a tool where you can review your account safety settings to strengthen your online security. 
[ Do an account check-up ](https://account.microsoft.com/account-checkup)
![Two green toggles in off and on positions.](https://cdn-dynmedia-1.microsoft.com/is/image/microsoftcorp/privacy_controls_card_809X461?wid=834&hei=470&fit=crop&resSharp=1)

Learn how to find your privacy settings and other related information in Microsoft products and services.
